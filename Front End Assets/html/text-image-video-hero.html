Our Mission

For At Risk Kids, Literacy Brings Hope.

When you give books to children, magic happens. Eyes sparkle, smiles emerge, and new possibilities come to light for the first time. Our mission is to inspire a life-changing love of learning through reading. We do this by providing a choice of new, high-quality, and culturally relevant books to kids who need them the most.
